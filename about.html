Talk2Me is the platform where influencers and their fans truly get to connect with each other

Talk2Me is built on the idea that influencers and fans should have no borders between them. We are built on the idea that there is no better way to influence someone than to deeply connect with them.

Who we are

Our founding

As first year students at McMaster University, we realized that authenticity is key in connecting with and influencing people. Talk2Me provides a platform where the noise of advertisements and the appeal of instant dopamine through likes and clicks are eliminated. A place where people can truly connect.

Growth & beyond

Currently, Talk2Me is a two person operation based out of Hamilton, ON. To accommodate the growing numbers of influencers and followers, Talk2Me is planning on expanding to a multi-team operation in the near future. Follow us on Talk2Me for future updates!
